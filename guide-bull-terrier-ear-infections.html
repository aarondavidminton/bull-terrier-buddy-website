Bull Terrier ear infection warning signs

Bull Terrier Buddy guide

Early tracking helps distinguish occasional irritation from recurring ear problems.

Track these signs

  • Head shaking frequency
  • Ear scratching side and intensity
  • Odor, redness, discharge type
  • Pain response during touch
  • Recent bathing/swimming events

Avoid home experimentation when pain, discharge, or strong odor is present.

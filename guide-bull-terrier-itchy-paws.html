Bull Terrier itchy paws guide

Bull Terrier Buddy guide

Itchy paws are common and often multi-factor. Track severity, timing, and exposures so trends become obvious.

What to log each day

  • Front vs back paw involvement
  • Licking frequency and intensity
  • Walk surface and weather
  • Food/treat changes in last 72 hours
  • Any topical or wash used

If licking escalates or skin breaks, contact your vet promptly.

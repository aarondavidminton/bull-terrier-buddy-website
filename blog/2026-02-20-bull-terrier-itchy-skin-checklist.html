Bull Terrier itchy skin checklist

20 February 2026

If your Bull Terrier has a flare week, structure matters more than guessing. Use this checklist to gather better evidence before the next vet conversation.

Track these five things daily

  • Body areas affected (paws, ears, belly, face, tail)
  • Itch intensity and timing
  • Food/treat changes in the last 72 hours
  • Environmental exposure changes (grass, cleaners, weather)
  • Topicals/medications used and response

Short, consistent notes usually outperform long occasional logs. For a full framework, see the allergy and skin tracking guide.

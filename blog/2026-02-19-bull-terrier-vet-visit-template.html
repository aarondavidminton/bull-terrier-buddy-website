Vet prep

A Bull Terrier vet visit template every owner can use

19 February 2026

Appointments become more useful when you bring concise context instead of memory-based summaries.

Bring this every appointment

  • Symptom start date and progression timeline
  • Medication list with dose adherence notes
  • Recent appetite/stool/activity changes
  • Two or three specific decision questions

This template reduces missed details and helps vets make faster decisions. Expand with the full vet prep guide.

Our story

Built for owners who want better consistency, not complexity.

Bull Terrier Buddy started from a simple problem: important details were spread across notes apps, calendars, and memory. We wanted one practical system for day-to-day bull terrier care.

Designed around daily owner workflows.

What we focus on

Clear records, steady routines, and useful context for veterinary conversations. No clutter and no hidden complexity.

What we value

Clarity, practical behavior tracking, and local-first data ownership. If a feature does not help an owner care better, it does not belong.

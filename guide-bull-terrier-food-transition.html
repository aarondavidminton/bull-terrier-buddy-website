Safe food transitions for sensitive Bull Terrier stomachs

Bull Terrier Buddy guide

Sudden food changes can cause avoidable stomach upset. Transition gradually and monitor response.

7-10 day transition template

  • Days 1-3: 75% old / 25% new
  • Days 4-6: 50% old / 50% new
  • Days 7-8: 25% old / 75% new
  • Days 9-10: 100% new if stable

Track during transition

  • Stool consistency
  • Appetite changes
  • Gas or itching changes

Pause or slow down if symptoms worsen.

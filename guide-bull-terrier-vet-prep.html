How to prepare for a Bull Terrier vet appointment

Bull Terrier Buddy guide

Good prep makes appointments more productive. Bring concise data, not scattered notes.

Bring this every time

  • Symptom timeline with first onset date
  • Current medication and adherence notes
  • Recent appetite/stool/energy changes
  • Any photos of visible flare-ups
  • Top 3 questions you need answered

Keeping these in one app view reduces missed details.

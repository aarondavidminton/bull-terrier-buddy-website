Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about setup, features, and privacy.

What is Bull Terrier Buddy?

It is a focused health and care tracking app for English and Miniature Bull Terriers, including routines, logs, vet costs, and food checks.

Does it require an account?

No account is required in the current version. Data is stored locally on your device.

Can I add my dog's photo?

Yes. You can choose a photo from your device gallery when creating or editing a profile.

Can I back up my data?

Yes. You can export and import backup JSON files through the app menu.

Does this replace veterinary advice?

No. Bull Terrier Buddy is a tracking and educational tool. It does not replace diagnosis or treatment from a qualified vet.

Where can I read your privacy policy?

See the privacy page for the current policy and data handling details.

Use a Bull Terrier behaviour journal effectively

Bull Terrier Buddy guide

Behaviour logs are strongest when they track context, not just incidents.

What to capture each time

  • Trigger context (who/what/where)
  • Intensity score
  • Duration and recovery time
  • What intervention you used
  • Outcome after intervention

Over 2-4 weeks, patterns often emerge around timing, environment, and stimulation load.

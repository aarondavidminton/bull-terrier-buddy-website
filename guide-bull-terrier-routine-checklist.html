Bull Terrier daily and weekly routine checklist

Bull Terrier Buddy guide

Bull Terriers do best with predictable structure. A routine keeps behaviour steadier and helps you catch health changes early.

Daily checklist

  • Feeding and water check at consistent times
  • Exercise block with intensity note
  • Quick stool/appetite observation
  • Medication check (if prescribed)
  • Two-minute behaviour note

Weekly checklist

  • Weight entry and body condition score
  • Skin/paw/ear check
  • Care plan completion review
  • Vet cost and medication updates

Start small. Consistency beats perfect detail.

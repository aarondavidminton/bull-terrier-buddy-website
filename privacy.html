Bull Terrier Buddy Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bull Terrier Buddy ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use the Bull Terrier Buddy mobile app and this website.

1. What Bull Terrier Buddy Stores

Bull Terrier Buddy is a local-first app. The app stores data on your device, including:

  • Dog profile information: name, breed, sex, birth date, coat color, microchip/KC/neutered status, allergies, and owner notes.
  • Health and care tracking data: exercise, weight/body condition, behavior, skin/coat, mobility, hearing, cardiac, medication, care plan items, and vet cost logs.
  • Food checker preferences: favorites and in-app settings such as unit and currency preferences.
  • Optional dog photo: if you choose one from your photo library.

2. What We Do Not Collect

  • No account sign-up is required in the current app version.
  • No personal identifiers are collected by us through a backend service.
  • No in-app analytics, ad tracking SDK, or crash-reporting SDK is integrated in the current app codebase.
  • No payment card details are collected by us in the current app version.

3. How Data Is Used

  • To power app features you choose to use, such as logs, reminders, profile views, and calculations.
  • To save and restore your data locally between app sessions.
  • To import/export backup files when you initiate backup or restore.

4. Permissions and Device Access

Bull Terrier Buddy may request:

  • Photo library access: only to let you select a dog photo for a profile.
  • File access: only for user-initiated backup export and restore import.

We do not access camera, microphone, contacts, or location in the current app code.

5. Backup Files

If you use the backup feature, your app data is exported to a JSON file at a location you choose. That file is under your control and is not automatically uploaded by us.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. In the current app architecture, app data stays on your device unless you manually export/share it yourself.

7. Data Retention

Data remains on your device until you delete it in-app, clear app data, uninstall the app, or overwrite it by importing a different backup.

8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards in app design, but no storage system is fully risk-free. You are responsible for protecting your device, backups, and any files you share.

9. Children’s Privacy

Bull Terrier Buddy is not directed to children under 13. Because the app is local-first and does not require account creation in its current version, we do not knowingly collect children’s personal data through a server.

10. Your Choices

  • Do not add optional data you do not want stored.
  • Remove data in-app, or uninstall the app to remove local app data.
  • Control backup files directly in your device/cloud storage accounts.

11. Changes to This Policy

If app functionality changes (for example, new cloud features), this policy will be updated before or at the time those changes are released.

12. Disclaimer

Bull Terrier Buddy provides general educational information and tracking tools for dog owners. It is not a substitute for professional veterinary advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified veterinarian for guidance about your dog's health, behavior, and treatment decisions. In emergencies, contact your veterinarian or local emergency animal hospital immediately.

Bull Terrier Buddy Privacy

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bull Terrier Buddy ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Bull Terrier Buddy mobile app and this website.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect:

  • Account and contact information: email address and profile details (if account sign-in is used).
  • Pet and usage data: routine schedules, health logs, notes, app settings, and in-app activity data.
  • Device and technical data: device type, OS version, app version, crash logs, and diagnostics.
  • Purchase information: subscription status and transaction metadata provided by app stores (not full payment card numbers).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve core app functionality.
  • Save your routines, logs, and preferences.
  • Troubleshoot bugs, monitor app performance, and prevent abuse.
  • Manage subscriptions and access to premium features.
  • Respond to support requests and important service notices.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, crash reporting, and customer support tools.
  • App marketplaces: Apple App Store and Google Play for purchases and compliance.
  • Legal requirements: where required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

4. Tracking and Analytics

We may use analytics and diagnostics to understand app performance and feature usage. Data is used in aggregate where possible and to improve reliability and user experience.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed to provide services, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion, subject to legal limitations.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. Children’s Privacy

Bull Terrier Buddy is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without verified parental consent.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Request a copy of your data where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border data transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date and, where required, provide additional notice within the app or website.

11. Disclaimer

Bull Terrier Buddy is an informational and organizational tool only. It does not provide veterinary diagnosis, treatment, or medical advice. Always consult a qualified veterinarian for professional guidance about your dog’s health, behavior, and treatment decisions. In emergencies, contact your veterinarian or local emergency animal hospital immediately.

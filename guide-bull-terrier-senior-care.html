Bull Terrier senior care checklist

Bull Terrier Buddy guide

Senior care works best when small changes are logged early.

Weekly senior check-ins

  • Mobility and stiffness trend
  • Appetite and hydration changes
  • Medication adherence
  • Sleep quality and restlessness
  • Comfort during walks

Documenting drift over weeks helps your vet adjust care plans faster.

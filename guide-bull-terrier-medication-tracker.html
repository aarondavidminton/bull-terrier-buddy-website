Bull Terrier medication tracker best practices

Bull Terrier Buddy guide

Medication records are most useful when they are simple and complete.

Log every dose with

  • Date and time
  • Dose amount
  • Given or missed status
  • Observed side effects

Weekly review

  • Missed dose count
  • Side effect pattern check
  • Questions for your next vet call

This makes treatment discussions accurate and objective.

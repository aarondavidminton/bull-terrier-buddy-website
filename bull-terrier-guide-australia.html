Bull Terrier care guide (Australia owners)

Heat-aware routine planning, recovery tracking, and skin monitoring for Australian owners.

In warmer periods, adjust exercise timing and track next-day recovery closely to avoid overload.

Warm-weather priorities

  • Exercise timing and intensity
  • Hydration and appetite notes
  • Paw/skin heat irritation checks
  • Recovery quality next morning

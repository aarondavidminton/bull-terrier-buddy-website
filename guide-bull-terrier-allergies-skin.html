Bull Terrier allergies and skin flare-up tracking

Bull Terrier Buddy guide

Bull Terriers can be prone to skin and allergy issues. Pattern tracking helps separate one-off irritation from repeat triggers.

Track these details together

  • Area affected: paws, ears, belly, face, tail
  • Symptom severity and itch level
  • Recent food changes or treats
  • Environment changes: walks, grass, cleaners, weather
  • Any medication or topical use

Short, consistent notes are more useful than occasional long entries.
